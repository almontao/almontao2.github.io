Here are the lectures for my ACTL 2015 course Syntactic universals in the lab: New methods and approaches

My research focuses on understanding how languages are shaped by learning and use. More specifically, I am interested in how typological universals (differences in the frequency of linguistic patterns across the world's languages) arise from properties of our cognitive system. To get at this, I teach people (children and adults) miniature artificial languages, and create computational models of their behaviour.
